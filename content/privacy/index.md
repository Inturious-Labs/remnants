## Information We Collect

When you subscribe to Remnants of Globalization, we collect your email address. That's it.

## How We Use Your Information

Your email address is used solely to send you newsletter updates when new stories are published. We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties.

## Email Service Provider

We use [Buttondown](https://buttondown.com) to manage our newsletter. When you subscribe, your email address is stored securely on their servers. Buttondown's privacy policy can be found at [buttondown.com/legal/privacy](https://buttondown.com/legal/privacy).

## Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. This collects anonymous usage data such as pages visited and time spent on site. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

## Cookies

This website uses minimal cookies necessary for basic functionality. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

## Unsubscribe

You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any email, or by contacting us directly.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via [@herbertyang](https://twitter.com/herbertyang) on X.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
